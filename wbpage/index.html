quarantine survival guide!

this site was made to help cure your boredom during quarantine! (and give some health tips)

wash your hands!

continuously wash your hands throughout the day! use soap and lather for 20 seconds.

wear a mask!

if you do have to go outside as a necessity, always wear a mask! this is to prevent the spread of germs between people.

please practice social distancing.

it might be tempting to go hang out with your friends during this time, but it's important to stay safe in the time of a global pandemic. stay home !!
